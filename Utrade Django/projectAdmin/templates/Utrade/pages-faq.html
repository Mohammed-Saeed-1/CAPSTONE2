Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Frequently Asked Questions
Portfolio and Plans
1. How do I create a new investment plan?

To create a new investment plan, navigate to the "Trade" section in your UTRADE account, where you can specify details such as stock symbols, end date, and the initial amount. Choose the stocks you're interested in, and you're all set!

2. Can I edit or cancel an existing plan?

No, you cannot edit or cancel an existing plan.

3. Where can I view my portfolio details?

Your portfolio details can be found in the "Portfolio" section. It provides a comprehensive overview of all your plans, including the initial amount, current total price, number of shares, and the outcome based on our algorithm. Additionally, you can view real-time information for each symbol separately.

4. How can I add the amount of money to my wallet?

To add money to your UTRADE wallet, visit the "Portfolio" section, click the "pencil" icon inside the wallet box, edit the amount, and save the changes.

Stock Information

The stock symbol is a unique abbreviation assigned to a publicly traded company. It serves as a shorthand representation of the company's name for stock market trading. For example, AAPL is the stock symbol for Apple Inc.

Utilize the search functionality in the "Stocks" section. Enter the stock symbol to retrieve relevant stock chart and information, including the logo, current price, volume, market cap, and more.

When viewing stock information in the “Stocks” section, click on the "Add to Favorites" button to include the stock in your list of favorites. This allows you to easily track and access information about stocks you are interested in.
Testing Plans
1. How does the testing feature work?

The testing feature in UTRADE allows you to simulate the performance of an investment plan. Enter details such as stock symbols, expected selling date, and the initial amount. You can manually split the amount or use the auto-filling option to maximize shares.

2. What is the purpose of the auto-filling option?

The auto-filling option automatically distributes the initial amount among selected stocks to maximize the number of shares based on our algorithm. It provides a convenient way to optimize your investment plan for potential profitability.

Algorithm and Predictions

UTRADE's prediction algorithm combines historical data, machine learning, and statistical techniques. It analyzes patterns, market trends, and factors influencing stock prices to provide real-time predictions for the performance of selected stocks within your plans.

The algorithm considers a variety of factors, including historical stock performance, market trends, news sentiment, and other relevant data points. It aims to provide accurate predictions by analyzing a comprehensive set of variables.

The accuracy of predictions can vary based on market conditions. UTRADE's algorithm is designed to continuously learn from historical data and adapt to changing market dynamics, enhancing its accuracy over time. It's important to note that it is not necessary for predictions to always be completely accurate, as the stock market inherently involves uncertainties and fluctuations.
Security and Privacy
1. Is my data shared with third parties?

No, we do not share it with third parties. We prioritize the privacy and security of your information.

Recommendations

Recommendations for splitting the initial amount are generated using an algorithm that assesses potential returns for each stock. The algorithm prioritizes allocating funds to stocks with the most promising expected profits, providing you with optimal recommendations for maximizing profitability.
Contact and Support
1. How can I get in touch with UTRADE support?

You can contact UTRADE support through our "Contact Us" section on the website. Alternatively, you can send an email to support@utrade.com for assistance.
